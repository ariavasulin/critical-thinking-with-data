# For Teachers

This page consolidates everything a teacher needs to adopt the curriculum. It is the entry point for instructors who want to teach *Critical Thinking with Data* in a classroom, a summer program, or as enrichment alongside AP Statistics.

## Alignment and credit

- **California AB 101** Ethnic Studies graduation requirement
- Candidate for **UC/CSU Area C mathematics** credit (UC recognizes data science as a valid math course)
- Designed to precede **Data 8**, **Data 104**, and **Data C4AC** at UC Berkeley — students complete the course fluent in Python, Jupyter, and the HCE Toolkit
